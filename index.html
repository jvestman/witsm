Benefits

  • No backend or shared platform required
  • Portable, offline-ready, client-side forms
  • Save filled forms as HTML files or generate shareable links
  • Send structured RFCs and notifications via email/chat
  • Generate .eml and .ics for email/calendar
  • Ideal for vendor collaboration and air-gapped environments
  • Lightweight process enablement for small or distributed teams
  • Maintain auditability and traceability with no overhead

How It Works

  1. Open a WitSM tool in your browser
  2. Fill out the form with structured data
  3. Save it locally, or share via link or download
  4. Send via email, chat, or calendar invite
  5. Others can open and collaborate ‚Äî no server needed
